# LoadIQ Product Charter

LoadIQ is a transportation profitability intelligence and decision-support platform.

LoadIQ helps operators understand profitability, operating costs, freight decision context, saved-load patterns, profile assumptions, and operational signals. It supports decisions. It does not make decisions for the operator.

## Non-Claims

LoadIQ is not:

- A broker
- A carrier
- A dispatcher
- An ELD
- A tax advisor
- A legal advisor
- A compliance authority
- An insurance advisor
- An accounting service
- A route authority
- A guaranteed-profit system

LoadIQ must not imply guaranteed savings, guaranteed freight quality, guaranteed compliance, certified routing, tax treatment, insurance advice, legal advice, or guaranteed profitability.

## Core Product Pillars

- Calculator
- Mileage
- Fuel
- Saved loads
- Fit Check
- Settings/profile
- Expense Intelligence
- Vehicle Intelligence
- Billing
- Atlas/AI
- Admin/internal tools
- Capacitor/mobile/iOS/Android surfaces

## Public Product Posture

Public copy may describe LoadIQ as decision support, profitability intelligence, freight estimate support, and operational awareness.

Public copy must not claim that all future provider integrations, mobile capabilities, AI systems, or tier capabilities are currently active unless the repo evidence and rendered app verification support that claim.
